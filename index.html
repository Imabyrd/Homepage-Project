About Harbor & Hearth

At Harbor & Hearth Design Co., we believe a home should be a haven. Our approach blends New England coastal serenity with warm, modern craft — creating spaces that feel peaceful, purposeful, and deeply personal.

Projects

The Windshore Cottage Renovation

A full coastal remodel featuring white oak floors, muted tones, linen textiles, and handcrafted cabinetry. The design focused on maximizing natural light and creating a calm retreat for a family of four.

Blue Harbor Café Branding

Complete visual identity and interior styling for a seaside espresso bar. Deliverables included logo, color palette, menus, signage, and seating layout that encourages slow, cozy coffee moments.

Lighthouse Loft Living Room

A modern New England loft with exposed beams, navy accent walls, and warm layered lighting. Custom shelving, built-in seating, and curated art bring the space together.

Driftwood Workspace Desk

Custom desk made from reclaimed driftwood and matte iron legs for a home office overlooking the harbor. Integrated cable management and discreet storage maintain a clean, focused workspace.

Meet The Team

Avery Collins – Creative Director
